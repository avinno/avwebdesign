Consulting

A web design consultant is an expert who provides professional advice to businesses on establishing an online presence and achieving successful results through web-related strategies. These consultants have extensive knowledge in various areas of web design and development, going beyond creating visually appealing websites. They guide businesses in optimizing their online potential and making informed decisions.

Design

Web design involves creating a website’s visuals, including color schemes, font choices, and page layouts1. It goes beyond aesthetics to include overall functionality, web apps, mobile apps, user interface (UI) design, and user experience (UX).

Core Components:

  • UI Design: Focuses on how elements look and interact with users.
  • UX Design: Aims to enhance user satisfaction by improving usability and accessibility.
  • HTML and CSS: The building blocks for structuring and styling web content.
Maintenance

Website maintenance involves performing tasks to keep your website functioning properly and up-to-date. Here’s why it’s crucial for your business:

  1. First Impressions: 87% of people conduct online research before making a purchase. If your website looks outdated or doesn’t work well, potential customers may click away and explore competitors’ sites.
  2. User Experience: A well-maintained site ensures a positive user experience89% of users who have a poor experience will shop elsewhere.
  3. Sales Growth: Your website guides users toward making purchases. For e-commerce sites, functionality is critical. Regular maintenance prevents missed sales opportunities.
